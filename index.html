Welcome to the world (Taylor's Version)

Let's first start by explaining the (Taylor's Version). This is simply what Taylor Swift uses at the end of her albums when she owns it. Indeed, a few years ago her record label sold her six first albums to a man, when she wanted to buy them too. She then changed her label and decided rerecording her albums with additional songs. You can now either listen to the "stolen" version of her albums, or to the version she owns. However, she still hasn't released all her rerecordings, we are still waiting for the (Taylor's Version) of repuation and Taylor Swift, her first and sixth albums.
